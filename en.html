About Us

Groupe Atlanta has been the choice of consumers and building professionals for doors and windows in the Greater Montreal Area since 1978. Groupe Atlanta's expanding line of products also includes patio and garage doors, railings and gates, stairs, balconies and awnings. An industry leader, Groupe Atlanta has built a reputation on the quality and originality of its products, highly competitive prices, and unbeatable customer service. For small or large projects – new constructions or renovations, our standard or custom-size solutions will match your budget, your aesthetic sensibilities, and your timeline.

Why Choose Groupe Atlanta?

Our reputation is founded on manufacturing excellence, product reliability, and customer satisfaction. Our price/value proposition is one of the best in the industry. Our products are tested in AIR-INS laboratories for energy performance, and they comply with ENERGY STAR requirements. Our job isn't done until you are entirely satisfied. You can rely on our team of certified professional installers and our after-sales customer service team.

Entry Doors

Lock out the elements and add style to your home with an aluminium door from Groupe Atlanta. We have doors to match your every need. Our residential and commercial doors are available in standard and custom sizes. They are equipped with door handles and locks by Weiser, a recognized industry leader and innovator - the first to offer Brilliance™, Lifetime Anti-Tarnish Finish, Powerbolt™ keyless electronic entry system, and Prestige™ distinctive forged solid brass locks. An Atlanta door and a Weiser door handle reflect refinement and style. Add elegance and beauty to your doors with decorated glass from Novatech®, Verre Select, Vitre-Art, ArtTech, or BayLite.

Security - All our doors are reinforced by an exclusive extruded aluminium solid rail system. Optionally, our doors come equipped with multipoint locks.

Insulation - Our doors are unsurpassed for airtightness, equipped with triple weatherstripping to greatly reduce the risk of heat loss.

Durability - From concept to finished product, by way of building materials, we take every detail into account to ensure the durability of our doors.

Energy Star - Our doors are independently tested and certified by ENERGY STAR, the symbol of energy efficiency, for heat transfer rate, energy rating, and airtightness.

LIMITED WARRANTY

Aluminium Doors and Windows

Groupe Atlanta Inc. offers a limited guarantee, to the original buyer, as described below. The company agrees to replace the defective parts as follow.

ALUMINUM

Groupe Atlanta Inc. guarantees its aluminium extrusions for a period of 25 years beginning from the delivery date. This guarantee does not cover gradual discolouring or staining on the surface as a result of sunshine, acid rain, or pollution. The duracron baked enamel paint finish is not covered by this guarantee since this is covered by our suppliers’ guarantees. Aluminium Atlanta Inc. guarantees that its profiles will maintain their general characteristics and will not rust or rot.

HARDWARE

Groupe Atlanta Inc. guarantees its hardware for a period of 5 years for casement windows and 5 years for sliding and double hung windows and patio doors. The guarantee does not cover oxidation or discoloration of the finish.

SCREENS

Groupe Atlanta Inc. guarantees its screens for 1 year. Groupe Atlanta Inc will replace any defective pieces excluding defects caused by normal wear and tear or abuse.

SEALED THERMO UNITS

Groupe Atlanta Inc. guarantees its thermo units for a period of 10 years following the delivery date. The guarantee covers defects resulting in condensation between the glass which obscures visibility and is due to a seal failure on the thermo unit and not the breakage of the thermo unit. A guarantee of 1 year covers the spontaneous breakage of a glass in a thermo unit. To be covered by this part of the guarantee, there should be no visible point of impact on the glass.

CONDITIONS

During the first 12 months of the guarantee, Groupe Atlanta Inc. will replace and install (labour included) all defective pieces be they aluminium, PVC, hardware, screens, or sealed units.

After the initial 12 months, Groupe Atlanta Inc. will supply any replacement pieces. Any necessary labour cost are not covered.

This guarantee does not cover any problem which occurs due to improper installation, abuse, lack of proper maintenance, an accident, fire, flooding or an inherent problem of the building which is out of our control.

Groupe Atlanta Inc. reserves the right to replace the defective parts with the original model or an equivalent Groupe Atlanta Inc. reserves the right to modify or improve its products without modifying products previously manufactured.

This warranty is valid only to the original purchaser (no transferable).
